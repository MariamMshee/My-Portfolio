World Happiness Score By Country

This project uses data that is compiled from the annual World Happiness Report, it draws primarily on data from the Gallup World Poll and includes key indicators such as GDP per capita, social support, healthy life expectancy, freedom to make life choices, generosity, and perceptions of corruption. The goal is to go beyond traditional economic measures like GDP and provide a broader understanding of what drives happiness and quality of life around the world.

Smart Agritech Kenya

Smart Agritech Kenya App is a climate-smart agriculture mobile solution designed to support smallholder farmers across Kenya. The app delivers timely, location-specific farming advice via SMS and USSD, ensuring accessibility even in low-internet areas. It provides weather forecasts, crop-specific guidance, pest and disease alerts, and best farming practices to help farmers make informed decisions, improve yields, and enhance resilience to climate change. By leveraging technology, the app empowers farmers to optimize their productivity and contribute to sustainable agriculture and food security.

Telco Customer Churn

The primary goal of this project is to help identify the factors that influence whether a customer is likely to leave (churn) or stay with the company. Key features include customer tenure, monthly charges, contract type, internet service, and whether they use services like online backup or tech support. This is meant to gain valuable insights into customer behavior, improve customer service strategies, and implement targeted interventions to reduce churn and support strategic decision-making in customer relationship management.
